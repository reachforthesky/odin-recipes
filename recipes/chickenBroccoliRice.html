Chicken Broccoli Rice Casserole

This recipe is a work in progress, if you make this according to these instructions it will probably come out weird and nasty.

This is a recipe for delicious and cheesy Chicken Broccoli Rice Casserole just like my mom used to make. This savory rice dish uses a few simple ingredients to great effect. This is an awesome choice to feed a few people or yourself for a few days!


Ingredients

  • 1lb. chicken breasts cut into bite sized chunks
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 3 cups of cheddar cheese shredded or diced into cubes
  • 3 cups of broccoli chopped into bit sized pieces
  • 2 cups uncooked rice

Directions

  1. preheat over to 400 degrees
  2. brown chicken in pan over high heat until golden brown
  3. Combine all ingredients into medium casserole dish and stir
  4. Cover casserole with tin foil
  5. cook casserole in oven for 30 minutes, stirring once or twice
  6. Take out and store or serve hot!
