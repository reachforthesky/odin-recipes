Beef and Bean Burritos

Burritos are my all-time favorite food medium, they combine many fantastic ingredients, yet they're so convenient to eat and make! All you really need to do is wrap some tasty stuff in a tortilla and enjoy! However, if you're like me and need things spelled out for you, here's my recipe for simple and tasty beef and bean burritos.


Ingredients

  • 1lb. ground beef
  • 1 can refried black beans
  • 6-8 tortillas (depends on size)
  • 1 packet of taco mix (good for 1lb. of beef)
  • shredded cheese

Directions

  1. Follow taco mix instructions to prepare ground beef
  2. Empty refried bean can into a small sauce pan and warm over low heat
  3. Combine taco meat and beans on individual tortillas
  4. Sprinkle shredded cheese over beef and beans to taste
  5. Wrap tortilla around other ingredients (You can store them for later)
  6. Microwave for about 30 seconds each and serve!
